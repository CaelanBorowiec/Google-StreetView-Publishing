Preparations

Viewpoint Planning

Plan your viewpoints before starting to take photos. Your view points should be near points of interest, doorways, and branch points. Viewpoints should typically be evenly spaced and layed out in straight lines.

Area Preparation

Prepare the area so that it appears how you want it to look in your finished tour. Tidy messy workspaces, remove clutter, etc. Set up any props you may want (posters, people, computers).

Gigapan Calibration

The suggested calibration settings for the Gigapan are:

  • Length: 100mm
  • Height: 56mm
  • Lens Focal Length (zoom): 11mm
